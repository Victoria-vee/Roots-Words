Chioma's Night Adventure

Mgbe anyanwụ dara, Chioma gara n'ubi iji were mkpụrụ.

When the sun set, Chioma went to the farm to fetch seeds.

N'ubi o hụrụ nnụnụ dị mma nke na-efe n'elu osisi.

In the field she saw a beautiful bird flying above a tree.

Nnụnụ ahụ kwụsịrị n'elu ogbe osisi ma kpọrọ Chioma ka ọ bịa nso.

The bird landed on a branch and invited Chioma to come closer.

Ha abụọ kparịrị maka otú esi ala nke ọma; Chioma mụtara otu okwu ọhụrụ.

They talked about caring for the land; Chioma learned a new word.

Mgbe mmiri malitere ịdọ, Chioma laghachiri n'ụlọ, were obi ụtọ kesaa ihe ọ mụtara.

When the rain began, Chioma returned home, happy to share what she had learned.

Nne ya jidere ya n'aka, gwara ya akụkọ banyere ubi nke nna nna ha, ma Chioma riri ụra n'enwe udo.

Her mother held her hand, told stories of their ancestors' farm, and Chioma slept peacefully.
