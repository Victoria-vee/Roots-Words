Sentences

Ndewo, kedu ka ị mere?

Hello, how are you doing?

Ụtụtụ ọma! Ị nụrụ ụra nke ọma?

Good morning! Did you sleep well?

Ehihie ọma, kedu otu ehihie gị si aga?

Good afternoon, how is your afternoon going?

Mgbede ọma, kedu ka ụbọchị gị si aga?

Good evening, how is your day?

Kedu? Ị dị mma?

How are you? Are you well?

Adị m mma, daalụ maka ịjụ.

I'm fine, thank you for asking.

Nnọọ! Bịakwa n'ụlọ anyị.

Welcome! Come into our home.

Daalu maka enyemaka gị.

Thank you for your help.

Biko, nweere m aka?

Please, can you help me?

Jisie ike! Ị na-eme nke ọma.

Keep it up! You are doing well.
